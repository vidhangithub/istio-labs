Circuit Breaker
The concept
Named after electrical circuit breakers. In electronics, when current exceeds safe levels, 
the breaker trips and cuts power — protecting the circuit. Same idea in software:

Normal state (CLOSED circuit — traffic flows):
productpage ──────────────────────► ratings ✅

Ratings starts failing/slow:
productpage ──────────────────────► ratings ❌❌❌

Circuit OPENS (trips) after threshold:
productpage ──X (instant fail)      ratings 🚫
             ↓
        returns error immediately
        instead of waiting

After cooldown period, circuit goes HALF-OPEN:
productpage ──── 1 test request ───► ratings
                 if succeeds → CLOSED again
                 if fails → OPEN again
