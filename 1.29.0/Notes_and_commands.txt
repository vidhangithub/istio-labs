Circuit Breaker
The concept
Named after electrical circuit breakers. In electronics, when current exceeds safe levels, 
the breaker trips and cuts power вҖ” protecting the circuit. Same idea in software:

Normal state (CLOSED circuit вҖ” traffic flows):
productpage в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв–ә ratings вң…

Ratings starts failing/slow:
productpage в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв–ә ratings вқҢвқҢвқҢ

Circuit OPENS (trips) after threshold:
productpage в”Җв”ҖX (instant fail)      ratings рҹҡ«
             вҶ“
        returns error immediately
        instead of waiting

After cooldown period, circuit goes HALF-OPEN:
productpage в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җ 1 test request в”Җв”Җв”Җв–ә ratings
                 if succeeds вҶ’ CLOSED again
                 if fails вҶ’ OPEN again
